Company Policies Overview
=========================

1. Working Hours
- Standard working hours are from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
- Flexible hours are allowed with manager approval, provided you complete 8 hours per day.

2. Remote Work
- Employees may work from home up to 2 days per week with prior approval.
- For critical releases or incidents, employees may be requested to be available outside regular hours.

3. Leave Policy
- Casual leave: 12 days per year.
- Sick leave: 10 days per year.
- Planned leave should be applied at least 3 days in advance when possible.

4. Data Security
- Do not share confidential company data outside authorized tools.
- Use strong passwords and enable multi-factor authentication where available.

5. Learning and Development
- Employees are encouraged to spend time learning new technologies relevant to their role.
- Courses, certifications, and internal projects (like LLM prototypes) can be proposed to your manager.
