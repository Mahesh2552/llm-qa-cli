Product FAQ
===========

Q1: What does our analytics product do?
A1: It collects data from multiple sources, cleans it, and provides dashboards and alerts to business users.

Q2: What kind of data sources are supported?
A2: We support relational databases, CSV files, Excel files, REST APIs, and selected cloud data warehouses.

Q3: How frequently is data refreshed?
A3: By default, data is refreshed every hour, but this can be configured per data pipeline.

Q4: Who is the target user?
A4: Business analysts, data engineers, and product managers who need quick insights from data.

Q5: How does the product handle sensitive data?
A5: Sensitive fields can be masked, encrypted, or excluded from reports based on data governance rules.
